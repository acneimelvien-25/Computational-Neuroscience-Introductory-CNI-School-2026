# 21-05-2026 Advances in Neurotechnology - Day 10


## Resource Overview

This session introduces participants to modern neurotechnology tools and open-access datasets used in computational neuroscience research, including fMRI, EEG, and calcium imaging data.

## Topics Covered

* Open-access neuroscience datasets
* Functional MRI (fMRI) data
* Electroencephalography (EEG) signals
* Calcium imaging techniques
* Translating data into research projects

---

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this session, participants should be able to:

* Identify major types of neuroscience datasets
* Understand how different neuroimaging methods capture brain activity
* Explore how datasets are used in computational research
* Develop initial ideas for research proposals

---

## Session Flow (What to Expect)

* Introductory lecture on neurotechnology and datasets
* Guided exploration of Neuromatch dataset resources
* Brainstorming session for research ideas
* Drafting of project proposals and presentation slides

---

## Preparation Note

Participants are encouraged to:

* Think about potential research questions early
* Review previous modeling and encoding sessions
* Be ready to collaborate on project ideas

---

##  Reflection Questions

* What types of brain data are most useful for research?
* How do different neuroimaging techniques differ in what they measure?
* What research question would you explore using open datasets?
