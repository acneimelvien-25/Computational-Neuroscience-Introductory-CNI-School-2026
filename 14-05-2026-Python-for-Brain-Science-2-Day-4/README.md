## Topics Covered

* Continued membrane potential behavior

By the end of this session, participants should be able to:

* Extend understanding of the LIF neuron model
* Explain how membrane potential evolves over time
* Describe spike generation in computational terms
* Relate simulation outputs to theoretical concepts
